# Talk2Me CRM v1.22.0 - Prospect management

- New prospects are automatically assigned to the staff member who captured them.
- Walk-in report shows inquiry category, question and action taken.
- Reports remain management-only; active prospect work is under Back Office > Clients > Prospects.
- Client Administration now includes tabs for All active, Prospects, Incomplete, Unassigned and Archived.
- Assigned consultant can be changed from the client list or client edit screen.
- Delete wording changed to Archive to preserve audit history.
