# Talk2Me CRM v1.21.0

## New Walk-in Contacts report
Back Office > Reports now shows potential clients captured from walk-ins/enquiries who were not previously in the database.

The report includes totals, conversion stages, staff member who captured the contact, contact type, assigned staff member and direct links to the client record.

## Client assignment
The client edit screen now includes Assigned staff member. Owner/admin/manager can update or remove the responsible staff member when saving the client record.

No database migration or NPM install is required.
