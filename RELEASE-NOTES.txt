Pest Workforce Platform v2.3.0 - Owner Changes and Legacy Parity

- Company Setup page combining company details, branding paths, attendance/GPS defaults and payroll settings.

- Leave / Absence report using active leave payroll adjustments.
- Period Totals by Staff Member printable one-page payroll attachment.
- Existing Time Adjustments retained with active/void audit handling.
- Working Lunch GPS evidence remains clickable in Sessions.
